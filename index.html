Driven to Make Change

ImmunoReach is a University of Calgary student-led organization. We are an interdisciplinary team of student leaders, researchers, and community advocates dedicated to translating complex immunological concepts into tangible action. By combining our diverse skills and perspectives, we aim to ensure that every community in Calgary has the tools and knowledge necessary to protect their health.

Our Work

We combine student-led research, community outreach, and care-based initiatives to support health awareness and dignity in Calgary.

Wellness Kit Building & Donation

Our team recently assembled and donated wellness kits for members of the Calgary community. Each kit contained essential hygiene supplies and a handwritten note of encouragement to promote dignity, wellbeing, and support.

What's In Each Wellness Kit?

Each kit was thoughtfully assembled by ImmunoReach volunteers and contains shampoo, soap, a razor, comb, toothbrush, toothpaste, and a handwritten message of encouragement.

Beyond providing practical supplies, the kits were designed to remind recipients that they are valued members of our community.
